Spring is one of the four seasons of the year, and it's characterized by warmer temperatures, blooming flowers, and longer days: 
        When it happens
        In the Northern Hemisphere, spring usually occurs from March 20 or 21 to June 21 or 22. In the Southern Hemisphere, spring usually occurs from September 22 or 23 to December 22 or 23. 
        What happens
        Temperatures gradually rise after winter, and plants and animals become active again. Birds migrate back north, and many baby animals are born.
